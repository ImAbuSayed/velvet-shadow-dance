[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Shadows falling on the hardwood floor
I have never wanted someone more
Pull you closer in the fading light
We are getting lost in the dark tonight

[Verse 1]
We took the long way down this quiet road
Leaving behind every heavy load
I see the firelight across your face
Falling into this slow and steady pace

[Pre-Chorus]
The whole world is sleeping outside my door
But all I can see is what I am reaching for
A little closer, taking it slow
We have got nowhere else to go

[Chorus]
This is our velvet shadow dance
Just you and me taking a chance
Heartbeats matching in the midnight glow
Take my hand and never let it go

[Whispered] (Our velvet shadow dance)

[Bridge]
